Help-Me-Fix-It

Many customers are calling to report problems with their laptops. The goal is to fix the customer’s problem by selecting two parts out of the available parts that are needed to fix the problem described.
You have a happy customer 😊 if the correct parts are selected to fix the customer's problem. You also earn 10 euros for fixing the problem!
On the other hand, wrongly selected parts do not fix the customer’s problem and you have a sad customer😞. You also lose 10 euros for each unhappy customer.
You win the game if you have at least 7 happy customers! Now let’s fix it!!!! 🧑‍💻🔧🪛🔩
